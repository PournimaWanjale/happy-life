Our Mission

At Happy Life, we are dedicated to providing support and resources for individuals struggling with mental health. We believe that no one should feel alone in their journey toward happiness and well-being. Our platform offers helpful resources, emergency contacts, and self-care tools to guide you every step of the way.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where mental health is given the same attention and care as physical health. We aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health, promote open conversations, and provide accessible tools for emotional and mental well-being.

Meet Our Team

Pournima Wanjale & Amanpreet Singh Walia - Founders

We are students of SY BBCA, passionate about mental health awareness. We started Happy Life with the goal of providing accessible support for everyone.
